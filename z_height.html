Height and Distance- General Questions

1.If an object travels at five feet per second, how many feet does it travel in one hour?

A.30

B.3000

C.18

D.1800

Option D

If an object travels at 5 feet per second it covers 5x60 feet in one minute, and 5x60x60 feet in one hour.
Answer = 1800

2.A vertical toy 18 cm long casts a shadow 8 cm long on the ground. At the same time a pole casts a shadow 48 m. long on the ground. Then find the height of the pole ?

A.1080 cm

B.180 m

C. 108 m

D.118 cm

Option C

At particular time for all object , ratio of height and shadow are same.
Let the height of the pole be 'H'
Then 18/8=H/48

H = 108 m.
