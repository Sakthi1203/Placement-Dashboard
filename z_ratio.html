Ratio and Proportion- General Questions

1. The salaries A, B, C are in the ratio 2 : 3 : 5. If the increments of 15%, 10% and 20% are allowed respectively in their salaries, then what will be new ratio of their salaries?

A. 3 : 3 : 10

B. 10 : 11 : 20

C. 23 : 33 : 60

D. Cannot be determined

Option C

Let A = 2k, B = 3k and C = 5k.
A's new salary = 115/100 of 2k = (115/100x 2k) = 23k/10
B's new salary = 110/100 of 3k = (110/100x 3k) = 33k/10
C's new salary = 120/100 of 5k = (120/100x 5k) = 6k
/ New ratio (23k/10:33k/10:6k) = 23:33:60

2.If 40% of a number is equal to two-third of another number, what is the ratio of first number to the second number?

A. 2:5

B. 3:7

C. 5:3

D. 7:3

Option C

Let 40% of A = 2/3 B
Then, 40A/100 = 2B/3
2A/5 = 2B/3
A/B= (2/3x 5/2) = 5/3
A : B = 5 : 3.

3. The fourth proportional to 5, 8, 15 is:

A. 18

B. 24

C. 19

D. 20

Option C

Let the fourth proportional to 5, 8, 15 be x.
Then, 5 : 8 : 15 : x
5x = (8 x 15)
x = (8 x 15)/5 = 24.

4.Two number are in the ratio 3 : 5. If 9 is subtracted from each, the new numbers are in the ratio 12 : 23. The smaller number is:

A. 27

B. 33

C. 49

D. 55

Option B

Let the numbers be 3x and 5x.
Then, 3x-9/5x-9 = 12/23
23(3x - 9) = 12(5x - 9)
9x = 99
x = 11.
The smaller number = (3 x 11) = 33.
