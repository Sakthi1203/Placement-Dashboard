  • Personal information
  • Educational background

  • Certifications
  • Completed projects

Group Discussion

Start the GD if you are very familiar with the subject.If you don't know the topic, rephrase the words that was spoken by another person.Attempt to either begin or end the GD. Avoid arguing with the other attendees.

Initiate

1. With all due respect, I would want to make the argument that...
2. Hello, ladies and gentlemen. I'd like to start off by saying that.
3. "I'd like to start by expressing my opinion on the matter..."

Agreeing

1. "I completely concur with."
2. "In actuality, X is correct in his assertion that..."
3. "I believe you are 100 percent correct"

Disagreeing

1. With all due respect, I can only partially concur with it.
2. "I don't think it would be right to..."

Opinion

1. I would like to emphasise that.
2. I'd like to state that.

Interrupt

1. "Let me just mention here that...
2. "Can I add something here, please?"
